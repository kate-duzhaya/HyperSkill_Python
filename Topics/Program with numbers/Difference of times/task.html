Difference of times

Megan decided to go for a walk, she knows the exact time when she went out and when she came back. Find out for how many seconds was she out for a walk.

The program gets the input of six integers, each on a separate line. The first three integers represent hours, minutes, and seconds of her departure, and the rest three integers define similarly when she returned. Print the number of seconds between these two moments of time.


Sample Input:
1
1
1
2
2
2

Sample Output:
3661


Sample Input:
1
2
30
1
3
20

Sample Output:
50



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 1 second
